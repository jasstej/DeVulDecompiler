FAQ

What is DeVul?

DeVul lets you upload a small binary and compare output from multiple open-source decompilers side by side.

How long do uploads take?

Simple samples are quick; heavier analyses (e.g., angr) can take a few minutes.

Is there a file size limit?

Yes, the UI enforces a ~2MB limit and the reverse proxy also limits request body size.
